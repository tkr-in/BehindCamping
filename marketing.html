3D AR Camera

Capture the outdoors in four specialized modes powered by ARKit. From star-filled skies to mapped terrain, every shot has depth.

  • Landscape, Terrain, Night, and Panorama modes

Nearby Camp Shops

An interactive map of everything around your campsite — gear stores, food suppliers, and medical facilities — with live status and navigation.
