What it does

Four tools every camper needs

01

Dry-Weather Forecast

7-day camping-specific weather with a safety score, rain chance, wind, humidity, and UV index for your campsite.

02

3D Camera

Capture landscapes, terrain, night skies, and panoramas using augmented reality depth. Photos save to your library.

03

Nearby Shops

Find gear, food, and medical stores around your campsite on a live map with ratings, hours, and navigation.

04

Traffic Radar

Live segment-by-segment traffic monitoring on your route home with jam alerts and alternate route suggestions.
