Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2025  ·  Last updated: June 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how BehindCamping ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the BehindCamping iOS application ("the App"). We take your privacy seriously. This policy is written plainly so you know exactly what we collect and why.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the App's features. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

  • Location data. The App requests your device location to show nearby shops on the map and to calculate traffic on your route home. Location data is used in real time and is not stored on our servers.
  • Camera and photos. The App accesses your camera for the 3D AR camera feature. Photos you capture are saved only to your own device photo library. We do not access or transmit your photos.
  • Motion data. The App uses motion sensors to improve AR terrain depth accuracy. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted.
  • Usage data. We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (such as which features are used most often) to improve the App. This data cannot identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App's four core features: weather, camera, shops map, and traffic.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs using anonymous diagnostics.
  • To respond to your support requests.

We do not use your information for advertising profiling, behavioral tracking, or any purpose not described above.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with the following categories of third parties only as necessary to operate the App:

  • Apple frameworks. The App uses Apple's MapKit, CoreLocation, and ARKit. Your use of these features is also subject to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy.
  • Weather data providers. Weather forecast requests may include your general geographic area (city or region level) to return relevant results. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Traffic data providers. Route and traffic data requests may include your origin and destination coordinates. This data is not stored or linked to your identity.

4. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers beyond what is required for a single session request. Support emails are retained only for as long as needed to resolve your issue.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. Because we retain minimal data, most requests can be fulfilled immediately.

You can revoke the App's access to location, camera, motion, and photo library at any time in your iPhone Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, contact us at:
